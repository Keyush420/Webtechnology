STREET FIGHTER

Street Fighter games are known for their fast-paced, 2D fighting gameplay, which involves players controlling a character and using a variety of attacks and special moves to defeat their opponents. The games have been released on a variety of platforms, including arcade cabinets, home consoles, and mobile devices. Street Fighter is considered one of the most influential and successful fighting game franchises of all time, and has inspired numerous spin-offs, sequels, and other media adaptations.
